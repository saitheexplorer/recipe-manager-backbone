Ingredients:

  • 3 cloves garlic

  • 1 large red onion

  • chilies to taste

  • 1 large tomato/3 small ones

  • white chickpeas/garbanzo beans (if you need to soak overnight, soak in warm water with a tiny bit of salt)
  • lemon juice
  • cilantro 
  • channa masala 
  • garam masala
  • chili powder
  • salt

method:

  1. chop all vegetable ingredients (fine chop garlic).

  2. heat a tiny bit of oil in a medium size pot.

  3. saute garlic first until light brown.

  4. add chopped onion and saute til browned.

  5. add chole, and keep lid on for faster cooking.

  6. stir often and add some water and salt.

  7. add chopped chillies.

  8. add garam masala, chili powder and channa masala to taste.

  9. keep stirring and let simmer.

  10. taste one chick pea to test if it has cooked.

  11. after the chole has cooked for some time, add tomatoes and add lemon juice to taste.
