Ingredients:

  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 3 carrots, sliced or grated
  • 3 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 15 ounce can hominy
  • 1 15 ounce can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp salt, or to taste
  • 1/4 tsp pepper, or to taste
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • fresh cilantro for garnish (optional)

Preparation:

In a large stock pot, simmer the garlic, onion and carrots in the water for a few minutes, until onions are soft. Add vegetable brothand allow to simmer for another 5 minutes or so, until carrots are soft.

Add remaining ingredients, and allow to simmer for at least 20 minutes. Garnish with a bit of chopped fresh cilantro, if desired.
