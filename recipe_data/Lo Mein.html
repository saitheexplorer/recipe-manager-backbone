Ingredients:
  • 1/2 lb fettuccine noodles or rice noodles 
  • 2-3 tablespoons soy sauce 
  • 1/4 cup oil 
  • 1 tbsp. sugar 
  • 1/4 tsp+ crushed red pepper 
  • 1/2 Red onion diced 
  • 1 jalapeno sliced 
  • 1 green onion stalk (optional) 
  • Moderate amount of parsely (optional)

Method: While noodles are boiling: In skillet over medium, heat oil and cook sliced jalapenos, green and red onions until tender and crisp, stirring quickly. Spoon spaghetti into saucepan. Add soy sauce, sugar and red pepper flakes (optional: add dry parsley) with spatula, toss gently to mix all ingredients well. Serve warm.
