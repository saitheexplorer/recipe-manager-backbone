8 oz Lo Mein noodles 
about 1 cup of a vegetable (I used edamame, but broccoli or peas or sugar snap peas would be tasty)
4 T brown sugar 
2 T lime juice 
4 T soy sauce 
1/4 t. red pepper
Olive oil 
2 garlic cloves, minced 
6 green onions, chopped

Mix brown sugar, lime juice, soy sauce and red pepper. Set aside. 

Cook noodles according to package instructions. You can either cook your vegetable in with the pasta in the last 5 minutes (super easy and no extra pot) or separately. Drain and rinse with cool water. 

Pour olive oil into the pasta pot and allow to heat on medium heat. Add garlic and cook for about 30 seconds. Pour in sauce mixture, then the lo mein/veggies. Stir to coat everything in the sauce. Add green onions and enjoy!
