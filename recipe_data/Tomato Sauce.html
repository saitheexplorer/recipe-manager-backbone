Ingredients

  • 1 can tomatoes or plum tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 5 tbsp butter
  • Large pinch salt

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a large saucepan over medium-high heat. Bring to a simmer, then lower the heat.
  2. Simmer for 45 minutes, crushing the tomatoes with the back of a wooden spoon as they soften.
  3. Remove and discard the onion (or use it as you like, see below).
  4. Serve over hot pasta topped with parmesan and black pepper.
