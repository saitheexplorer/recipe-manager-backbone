  • Mushrooms
  • Onions or scallots
  • Heavy cream
  • Stock
  • Pepper
First, cut up all the carrots, fennel, onions and mushrooms. Fry them for around 8 minutes. When they look fried and nice bring to medium heat and start slowly adding stock. Let it cook for about 15-20 minutes. Now, add heavy cream and turn up the heat. Keep stiring and add a good amount of pepper. After about 1 minut of this you are done. Always taste before serving.
