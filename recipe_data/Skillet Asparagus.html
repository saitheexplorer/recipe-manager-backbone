Ingredients

  • Asparagus (6–8 spears per person will do)
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic (1–2 cloves)
  • Salt (optional)
  • Lemon juice (optional)

Instructions

  1. Add 1 tbsp of olive oil to a medium-sized pan over medium heat.
  2. Mince or press the garlic and add it to the pan.
  3. Snap the woody ends off the asparagus spears if necessary. Cut the spears into thirds.
  4. Place the asparagus in the pan and cook for 3–5 minutes until it turns a brighter green colour.
  5. Serve seasoned with salt and a sprinkling of lemon juice.
