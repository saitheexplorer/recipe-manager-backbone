Ingredients

  • 1 (15 oz) can tomato sauce
  • ¼ cup water
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • ¼ tsp dried oregano
  • ¼ tsp dried basil
  • ¼ tsp dried thyme
  • ¼ tsp garlic powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ⅛ tsp pepper
  • 1 bay leaf
  • ¼ tsp lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Put all ingredients into a small sauce pan.
  2. Bring to a boil over medium heat, reduce heat and let simmer for 45 minutes until reduced and thickened.
